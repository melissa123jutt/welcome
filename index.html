The Boosting Project of Greater Manchester Chamber of Commerce

EcomBoost Hub is proud to be a key initiative under the "Boosting Project" by the Greater Manchester Chamber of Commerce. This groundbreaking project focuses on accelerating e-commerce growth and financial inclusion by empowering individuals to earn through global platforms like Amazon, Alibaba, and AliExpress.

Led by an experienced team, including Clive Memmott - Chief Executive and Phil Cusack - Chairman, this initiative brings innovation and economic opportunity to communities worldwide.

By aligning with Manchester's e-commerce objectives, EcomBoost Hub transforms how people interact with global markets, making financial independence a reality for all.
